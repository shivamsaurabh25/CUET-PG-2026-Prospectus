Results & Normalization

Ensuring fairness across multi-shift examinations

‚öñÔ∏è Why Normalization?

Since the difficulty level might vary across sessions, NTA uses the Equi-percentile Method. This converts raw marks into a relative score.

The Normalization Formula
$$ \text{Percentile} = \frac{100 \times \text{No. of candidates with raw score } \le \text{ yours}}{\text{Total candidates in that shift}} $$

Calculated to 7 decimal places to prevent ties.

01
Raw Score Calculation Marks are awarded: +4 for correct, -1 for incorrect. Max marks: 300.
02
Shift-wise Ranking Candidates are ranked within their specific shift based on their Raw Scores.
03
NTA Score (Percentile) The formula converts your rank into a percentile, which becomes your final admission merit.
