## üìù Important Notes for Candidates

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Normalization Rule:** Candidates appearing in different shifts will have their scores normalized. The final admission is based on the **NTA Score (Percentile)**, not the Raw Marks.

> [!WARNING]
> **Geolocation Rule:** You must choose exam cities that match your **Permanent** or **Present** address states provided in the application.
